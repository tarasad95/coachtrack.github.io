Coach Track — Support

We're here to help.

Coach Track helps fitness and CrossFit coaches manage clients, log workout results, and track progress. If you have a question, found a bug, or have a feature request, please get in touch.

Email us at tarasad95@gmail.com. We typically reply within a couple of business days.

Common questions

  • How do I reset my password? On the sign-in screen tap “Forgot password?”, enter your email, and follow the link we send you.
  • How do I add a client to several groups? Open the client's ⋮ menu on the home screen and tap each group to toggle membership.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
  • Which languages are supported? Ukrainian and English — switch anytime in Profile.
