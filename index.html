Built for coaches who care about progress

A closer look at how Coach Track fits into your week.

Coach Track is made for strength and conditioning coaches who run programs for many athletes at once. Instead of juggling notebooks and spreadsheets, you keep your entire roster, every logged result, and each athlete's history in one fast app that lives in your pocket — ready between sets, at the whiteboard, or after a session.

Logging is quick and flexible: record lifts, times, reps, rounds and more, with sets×reps, notes and a photo. Your most-used activities surface first and autocomplete as you type, so a full session takes seconds. Every entry feeds automatic personal bests and a clean trend chart, so you and your athletes can actually see the progress.

And it stays out of your way: a focused, one-handed interface, two languages, light and dark themes, and privacy by design — your data belongs to you, and you can remove it at any time.
